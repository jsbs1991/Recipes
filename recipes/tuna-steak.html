Awesome Tuna Steak

This is the perfect tuna for a nice dinner. It is't difficult and can be made fairly easily. It can be cooked in less then 20 minutes. It also does not have a long list of ingredients to find or buy.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 inch thick Tuna Steak
  • Salt 1/2 teaspoon
  • Pepper 1/2 teaspoon
  • Olive oil 2 tblspoons
  • Sesame seeds 2 tblspoons
  • Soy Sauce 2 tblsoons
    •
  1. First let the tuna sit out and reach room temperature. This is important to get an even cook.
  2. Get a bowl and add the ingredients together to make a marinade.
  3. Once the tuna has reached room temp let it sit in the marinade for ten minutes to soak in.
  4. While the tuna is marinading put a thick bottom pan on the stove on high.
  5. Place the tuna in the pan and cook on both side, two minutes per side.
